About Nairobi Oasis

Born from a belief that professional wellness care should reach everyone, wherever they are.

Our Mission

Wellness without boundaries

Nairobi Oasis was founded with a simple but powerful idea: the highest quality therapeutic massage should not require a trip to an expensive spa. It should come to you.

We make luxury wellness accessible, convenient and personalized by connecting clients with trusted wellness professionals wherever and whenever they need them.

Every partnership we form with a therapist is built on shared values: clinical excellence, genuine care, and unwavering professionalism. When our therapists arrive at your door, you're not just getting a massage. You're getting peace of mind.
